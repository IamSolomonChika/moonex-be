## Your First Trade

### Preparing for Trading

**Fund Your Wallet:**
1. **Buy BNB** on centralized exchange (Binance, Coinbase, etc.)
2. **Transfer BNB** to your wallet address
3. **Wait for confirmation** (usually 1-2 minutes on BSC)
4. **Check balance** in your wallet

**Recommended Starting Amount:**
- **New Users:** $10-50 worth of BNB
- **Experienced Users:** Based on your risk tolerance
- **Gas Fees:** Keep 0.01-0.02 BNB for gas

### Making Your First Swap

**Step 1: Navigate to Trade**
1. Click "Trade" in the main navigation
2. Select "Swap" from the dropdown
3. Choose your trading pair

**Step 2: Select Tokens**
- **From Token:** What you're selling (e.g., BNB)
- **To Token:** What you're buying (e.g., BUSD, CAKE)
- **Amount:** Enter the amount you want to swap

**Step 3: Review Quote**
- **Exchange Rate:** Check current market rate
- **Price Impact:** How much your trade affects the price
- **Gas Fee:** Estimated transaction cost
- **Minimum Received:** Guaranteed minimum amount
- **Slippage Tolerance:** Acceptable price change

**Step 4: Execute Trade**
1. Click "Swap" button
2. Review transaction details in your wallet
3. Approve the transaction
4. Wait for confirmation (usually 3-5 seconds on BSC)
5. Success! Check your new balance

### Pro Tips for Your First Trade

**Start Small:**
- Begin with small amounts to learn the process
- Understand gas fees and timing
- Get comfortable with the interface

**Check Prices:**
- Compare with other DEXs (if needed)
- Consider price impact for larger trades
- Monitor gas prices for optimal timing

**Save on Gas:**
- Trade during low-traffic times
- Use our gas optimization features
- Consider batching multiple trades

## Security Best Practices

### Wallet Security

**Seed Phrase Protection:**
- Write down your seed phrase on paper
- Store it in multiple secure locations
- Never share it with anyone
- Avoid digital storage (photos, cloud storage)
- Consider using a fireproof safe

**Private Key Security:**
- Never share your private key
- Use hardware wallets for large amounts
- Keep software updated
- Use secure devices only
- Be wary of phishing attempts

**Device Security:**
- Use antivirus software
- Keep your OS updated
- Use secure Wi-Fi networks
- Avoid public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Enable device encryption

### Platform Security

**Account Security:**
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Regularly update your password
- Be cautious of phishing emails
- Log out from shared devices

**Transaction Security:**
- Always verify transaction details
- Check recipient addresses carefully
- Start with small test transactions
- Use our MEV protection features
- Monitor transaction confirmations

**Browser Security:**
- Use secure, updated browsers
- Enable phishing protection
- Use official wallet extensions
- Bookmark official websites
- Be wary of browser pop-ups

### Common Scams to Avoid

**Phishing Scams:**
- Fake emails asking for private keys
- Fake websites impersonating our platform
- Social media scams offering "free tokens"
- Fake customer support requests

**Rug Pulls:**
- Research tokens before trading
- Check liquidity and trading volume
- Verify token contract addresses
- Be wary of unrealistic promises
- Use our token verification features

**Impersonation Scams:**
- Verify social media accounts are official
- Check Discord/Telegram server authenticity
- Be cautious of DMs from "admins"
- Never share sensitive information
- Report suspicious accounts
